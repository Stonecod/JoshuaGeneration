About Our Church

RCCG, Joshua Generation Parish is a Bible-believing church committed to raising people who love God and impact their community.

Mission & Vision

  1. To make heaven
  2. To take as many people with us
  3. To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations
  4. To accomplish No.1 above, holiness will be our lifestyle
  5. To accomplish No. 2 and 3 above, we will plant churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town of developing countries and withing five minutes driving distance in every city and town of developed countries.
  6. We will pursue these objectives until every Nation in the world is reached for the Lord Jesus Christ
